### 🤖 What I work on
 
I build **perception and control systems for embodied agents**. After first-author work on facial-behavior and vision-language models, I'm now focused on **humanoid locomotion and manipulation** — training policies on the Unitree G1 with diffusion / flow-matching VLA models.
 
- 🔭 Currently: training humanoid policies (Ψ₀ / Psi-Zero, Unitree G1, MuJoCo) @ USC PSI Lab
- 🧠 Interests: embodied AI · VLA models · vision-language models · efficient perception
- 🎓 Heading to **University of Michigan** for my MS in CSE (Fall 2026)
